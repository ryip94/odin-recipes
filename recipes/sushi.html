Recipe for a quick and easy Spicy Tuna Roll

This Spicy Tuna Roll is certain to be a refreshing and delicious meal that anyone with the willingness to learn will be able to make. It is a great meal for 1 to 2 people and healthy for you as well.

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Nori
  • Sashimi Grade Tuna
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Spicy Mayo

Steps

  1. Roll out the cooked rice onto a plastic-wrapped sushi roller
  2. Add a sheet of Nori on top of the rice
  3. Spead a light layer of Spicy Tuna onto the Nori
  4. Roll all ingredients together
  5. Top with a light spinkle of sesame seeds
